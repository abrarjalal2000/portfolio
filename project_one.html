"No More Monster Here" Stage Design Collaboration

Collaborated with Meghan Schlageter and Lily Telegdy
Stage Design I taught by Professor Crystal Tiala
Spring 2020

In this project for my stage design class my group and I have developed a script breakdown for the play "No More Monsters Here" by Marcus Gardley. We have conducted visual and thematic research for this play by creating a pinterest board and exploring unqiue and creative approaches to stage design.

For our final design we have decided to go with a surrealistic approach to the set design. This entails a more dream like effect on our play. We hope to acheive this with a pixelated videogame-like backdrop and exaggerated actions.
